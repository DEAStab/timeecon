The dead time

Every wait is a little pool of wasted attention.

You hit send, and the spinner spins — a few seconds, every single time. On its own it's nothing. But multiply it across every prompt, every day, across millions of interactions, and it becomes an ocean of idle, intelligent attention quietly draining into a loading animation.

Meanwhile treats that gap as the most reclaimable moment you have. Not empty time to tolerate — the opening move of a better conversation.

How it works

The moment you hit send, Meanwhile gets to work too.

It fires a second, fast plan in parallel with your main answer — turning the wait into two kinds of momentum, with nothing for you to set up.

  1. 01

    Fires in parallel

    The instant you send a prompt, Meanwhile launches a second, fast plan alongside your main answer — so the wait is already working before the spinner finishes its first turn.

  2. 02

    Streams a related read

    A focused, adjacent read appears beside your answer — the background you'd look up next, the consideration you hadn't framed, the obvious objection to your own question. Not a generic "did you know."

  3. 03

    Stages your next question

    One-tap prep questions compose a sharper follow-up as you answer them — so by the time the main answer lands, your next, better question is already written and ready to fire.

What makes it solid

Provider-agnostic

Your main answer streams from whatever model you already use. Meanwhile runs the wait-plan on a separate, fast model — it sits beside your stack, it doesn't replace it.

Measured, not guessed

An evaluation harness scores how relevant each read and question is — against the very same prompts the live app runs. Any improvement you can measure is, by construction, the one that ships.

Lives where you work

A standalone web app shows the full experience — and a Chrome extension docks Meanwhile right inside Claude.ai, in an isolated card that never interferes with the page.

Under the hood

Deliberately small. Provider-agnostic. Measured.

Meanwhile lives in the workflow you already have. The value is decided by a single file of prompts — and proven by the same prompts in evaluation.

01

One file of prompts is the source of truth

Everything the model is told lives in one place. Change the value the product delivers by changing the prompts — no sprawling, hidden logic to reason about.

02

Fast by design

A fast, cheap model (Haiku) generates the wait-plan and the related read so they arrive almost instantly — filling the panel before you'd even reach for another tab.

03

An eval harness keeps it honest

Relevance is scored against the exact prompts the live app uses, so what improves in evaluation is precisely what improves in production. No drift between the test and the thing you ship.

04

Two ways to run it

A full standalone web app that demonstrates the experience end to end — and a market-ready Chrome extension that injects Meanwhile into Claude.ai via an isolated shadow-DOM card.
